Meet Team Sky and Sea

Felicia Doswell, PhD is an associate professor and Interim Chair at Norfolk State University in the Department of Computer Science. With five years of research under her belt, she continues to work in that field of expertise regarding information assurance, networking, and human computer interactions. Moreover, Felicia Doswell, PhD looks into increasing digital access and computer education, and she is also an advocate for underrepresented students in subjects such as high performance computing. She teaches a variety of classes at Norfolk State University, with interests ranging from artificial intelligence to mobile device security.
